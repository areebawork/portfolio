Brand Audit

Apple — Still Dominant, Not Untouchable

An independent brand audit evaluating Apple's positioning, from the 1984 launch film and "Shot on iPhone" to rising prices and a $250M AI-marketing settlement.

  • India chapter: ~55M iPhones assembled in India in 2025 — roughly a quarter of global production.

  • Recommendation: market features only when ready; simplify an increasingly saturated ecosystem.

Brand Partnership Pitch

PRH India × Blue Tokai — "Brewing Stories"

A brand-collaboration proposal pairing new Penguin Random House releases with Blue Tokai's café network — turning a daily coffee habit into a book-discovery moment.

  • The insight: cafés are a habit, bookstores are a decision — pairing them turns everyday footfall into book discovery.
  • The mechanic: book-and-drink pairings, QR-linked author clips on cups, and an in-café PRH reading corner.
  • Rollout: a 4-week arc — micro-blogger teasers → influencer launch → UGC push (#BrewingStories) → data & press recap.
  • Pilot scope: Delhi, Mumbai & Bangalore, across 15–20 Blue Tokai locations, targeting the audience both brands already share: students & young professionals, 18–30.
  • Targets: 10% café footfall lift, 100+ organic #BrewingStories posts, 500+ QR scans within 4 weeks.
